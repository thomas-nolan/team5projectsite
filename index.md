![Game Screenshot](docs/Game-photo.png)

Trapped in a university you are tasked to escape, but it's not that easy. With tricky traps and a monster dean gaurding the only key card to escape its no easy task to Escape From Uni.
We're not all mean, there are some positive events as well as some hidden events for you to try and uncover.
